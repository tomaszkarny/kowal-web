What we do

Artistic Blacksmithing Workshop - Tadeusz Karny - offer wide range of products

Gates, railings, balustrades (interior, exterior), hand-made ornaments, decorative elements (candle holders, furniture) and many more. We offer various colors on client's demand (e.g. old gold, silver etc). All products are coated with anti-corrosion layers.

We provide our services to individual customers, as well as firms and institutions.
We will do our best to meet your expectations!

Artistic Blacksmithing Workshop

The Artistic Blacksmithing Workshop was founded in 1993. The owner - Tadeusz Karny had gained broad experience working for the Art Conservation Workshop in Bialystok, Poland. Nowadays he utilises his skills running his own company.

Our products decorate following places, companies, and institutions: Hasbach's Palace in Bialystok, Stanislaw Staszic College of Public Administration Bialystok, Voivodship Council in Bialystok, MPEC Main Office, Birk-Bud Main Office, University of Bialystok.
